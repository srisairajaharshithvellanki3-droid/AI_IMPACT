This data explores the potential impact of Artificial Intelligence on the global job market by the year 2030. It includes structured insights on how automation, machine learning, and AI-driven systems may transform industries, replace certain job roles, and create new career opportunities. 

The dataset is designed for data science learners, researchers, and Kaggle enthusiasts who are interested in future workforce trends, economic shifts, and AI adoption patterns.

Key focus areas include:

Job roles most affected by AI automation New careers emerging due to AI technologies Industry-wise impact analysis Skill demand changes by 2030 Regional and global employment trends

This dataset can be used for exploratory data analysis (EDA), predictive modeling, visualization projects, and research on future job market transformations.
